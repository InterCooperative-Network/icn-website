Why Choose ICN?

ICN provides the foundational infrastructure for a new kind of digital economy—one that's owned and governed by the communities that use it.

Federated Computing

Distributed mesh computing with automated job bidding, execution, and verification across the network.

Execute computational tasks across a decentralized network of nodes with built-in reputation systems and economic incentives.

Regenerative Economics

Sustainable mana-based resource system that regenerates over time, preventing abuse while ensuring fair access.

Our unique economic model uses regenerating capacity credits influenced by reputation and community policies.

Community Governance

Transparent, verifiable governance through formal proposals, voting mechanisms, and smart policy enforcement.

Participate in federated decision-making with explicit, versioned policies compiled to verifiable WASM contracts.

Decentralized Identity

Self-sovereign identity for individuals, cooperatives, communities, and federations using DIDs.

Manage your digital identity without relying on centralized authorities or corporate platforms.

Verifiable Execution

Every significant action produces signed receipts anchored in a content-addressable DAG.

Complete audit trail and transparency through cryptographically verifiable execution receipts.

Open Source

100% open source infrastructure built by and for the community, not corporate interests.

Modern Rust implementation with comprehensive documentation and contributor-friendly development practices.
