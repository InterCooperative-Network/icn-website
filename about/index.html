About the InterCooperative Network (ICN)

The Problem We Address

Today's internet, despite its potential for connection and empowerment, is largely dominated by centralized platforms. These digital monopolies often prioritize profit over user well-being, leading to issues like data exploitation, censorship, algorithmic manipulation, and a stifling of innovation. The promise of a truly open, democratic, and user-controlled digital commons remains largely unfulfilled.

We believe that the foundational infrastructure of our digital lives should not be in the hands of a few, but rather stewarded by the communities that depend on it.

Our Vision: The ICN Solution

The InterCooperative Network (ICN) is a bold initiative to build a new layer of the internet â€“ a decentralized, cooperative digital infrastructure owned and governed by its users. We envision a network of interconnected, autonomous cooperatives that provide essential digital services (storage, communication, computation, etc.) based on principles of mutual aid, democratic governance, and shared prosperity.

ICN aims to be a resilient, censorship-resistant, and equitable alternative to the centralized web, empowering individuals and communities to reclaim their digital sovereignty.

Our Guiding Principles

  • Cooperative Ownership & Democratic Governance: Network infrastructure and services are owned and controlled by member cooperatives, operating on one-member-one-vote principles. No monarchs, no bosses.
  • Transparency & Openness: All core protocols, software, and governance processes are open source and publicly auditable.
  • Subsidiarity & Federation: Autonomous cooperatives manage their own affairs while federating for greater network strength and resilience.
  • Mutual Aid & Solidarity: Cooperatives support each other, fostering a resilient and collaborative ecosystem.
  • User-Centricity & Data Sovereignty: Users control their data and digital identity. Services are designed for user benefit, not exploitation.
  • Ecological Sustainability: We strive to build and promote an internet that is environmentally responsible.
  • Cooperative Prosperity: Value generated by the network is distributed equitably among its participants, not extracted by external shareholders.

The People Behind ICN

ICN is a growing global community of technologists, activists, cooperators, and dreamers passionate about building a better digital future. While we don't have a traditional hierarchical structure, our efforts are driven by dedicated contributors and member organizations who believe in the power of cooperation.

We are a do-ocracy. The best way to see who is involved is to join our community channels and see who is building!
