Our First Blog Post!

Posted on July 26, 2024 by The ICN Team

Welcome to the official blog of the InterCooperative Network (ICN)! We're thrilled to launch this space where we can share our journey, insights, developments, and connect with our growing community.

What to Expect

Here, you'll find a variety of content, including:

  • Project Updates: Stay informed about the progress of key ICN initiatives like our wallet, AgoraNet, decentralized storage solutions, and more.
  • Technical Deep Dives: For the more technically inclined, we'll explore the architecture, protocols, and codebases that power ICN.
  • Community Spotlights: Highlighting the amazing contributions and individuals within our network.
  • Governance Discussions: Updates on governance proposals, meetings, and how you can participate.
  • Vision & Strategy: Thoughts on the future of the decentralized web, cooperativism in the digital age, and ICN's role in it.
  • Event Recaps & Announcements: Don't miss out on important events, AMAs, or community calls.

Join the Conversation

This blog is not just a one-way street. We encourage you to engage with the posts, share your thoughts in our community channels (especially our Matrix space), and let us know what topics you'd like to see covered.

The InterCooperative Network is built by and for its community. Your participation, feedback, and contributions are vital to our success.

Stay tuned for more posts soon. We're just getting started!
